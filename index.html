About the Theory

MU-CFT (Mandrov Unified Coherent Field Theory) is a comprehensive theoretical framework integrating quantum mechanics, general relativity, information theory, and consciousness through the principle of subjective coherence. It expands upon Mandrov Coherent Field Theory (MCFT) to incorporate dynamic observer-dependent fields and global coherence mechanisms.

"MU-CFT is not a theory of everything — it is a mirror of how everything holds together."
